About ALC

Building skills and bridging gaps

Accelerated Learning Centers (ALC) equips teens and adults in Chuuk with practical computer literacy— email, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, and Excel—through patient, hands-on instruction. Learners gain the confidence to communicate, create documents and presentations, and manage simple budgets. Recognized by Chuuk State on July 18, 2025, ALC complements existing church and community efforts.
